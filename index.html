direct-img.link

Live images in markdown, powered by search.

This service is free to use. However, API and infrastructure costs are significant. Without community donations, this project will die quickly.

Usage

Give your AI a system instruction to embed images using this service. No uploads, no APIs, no tokens.

![orange cat](https://direct-img.link/orange+cat)
![sunset at beach](https://direct-img.link/sunset+at+beach)

Caching

Images are cached for 30 days in R2 for instant delivery.

Privacy

No tracking. Queries are normalized and hashed.
